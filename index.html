Is this Suicide or Murder?

"The man who made the nation laugh is gone."

ACTOR NANHA COMMITS SUICIDE

The Final Note: "I am dying of my own free will; no one is to be held responsible."

LAHORE (Crime Reporter) – The legendary film star and comedian, Rafi Khawar, popularly known as "Nanha," has tragically ended his life. Reports indicate that the actor was suffering from severe depression following a domestic dispute regarding his second marriage to actress Nazli. Family sources revealed that his family's refusal to accept the marriage had caused him immense mental anguish.

The incident occurred early this morning when family members heard a gunshot ring out from the lounge. Upon rushing to the scene, they discovered the beloved actor slumped on a sofa, lifeless. He had reportedly used his own shotgun to commit the act. Just days prior, he had renewed his gun license, assuring his mother that the weapon was solely for home protection. Film studios across Lahore have announced a closure in mourning.

"I WON'T EVEN SEE HIM ONE LAST TIME"

Actress Nazli Weeps Inconsolably

LAHORE – Upon receiving the news, actress Nazli was reportedly in a state of shock. Speaking through tears, she expressed her disbelief, stating, "I cannot fathom why he would take such a step; he shouldn't have done this."

She lamented the circumstances that have now created a permanent distance between them. "The greatest regret of my life will be that I cannot even see his face one last time," she told reporters, highlighting the severity of the family conflict that now bars her from the funeral proceedings.

Police Initiate Investigation: Wife and Son Questioned

Authorities have seized the automatic rifle and ammunition magazine found near the body. The investigation has now widened to include statements from Nanha's first wife and his son. Police are trying to reconstruct the events leading up to the suicide to determine if there was any foul play or external incitement involved.

  • Nanha was deeply in love with Nazli, but sources claim she had grown indifferent recently.
  • This was reportedly not his first attempt to end his life.
  • Family disputes regarding property and marriage created a high-pressure environment.
